Scholarships

There are three popular football scholarships:

~ The Walter Byers Scholarship: this is a renewable postgrad scholarship. Annual awards are $24,000

~ The Jim McKay Scholarship: this is a $10,000 one time award.

~ The NCAA Postgraduate Scholarship: this is a $7,5000 one time award.
